Former Skeem Saam Star Cornet Mamabolo Opens Nature Resort in Limpopo Called Mabodibane Resort

  • Actor-turned-businessman Cornet Mamabolo has officially opened his nature resort and spoke about the inspiration behind it exclusively to Briefly News
  • The former Skeem Saam star opened a chill site in Limpopo called Mabodibane Resort
  • The official opening will be on 15 December, where a stall will be available to be booked for R300

Cornet Mamabolo is about to enjoy the fruits of his labour. The actor, who is now a fully-fledged businessman, owns a nature resort.

Actor opens nature resort

Cornet Mamabolo has officially opened his nature resort in Limpopo called Mabodibane Resort. The former Skeem Saam star has embarked on a media tour where he advertises his new business venture.

The resort will open its doors on 15 December, where a stall can be booked for R300. Announcing the news, Cornet wrote:

"E boima ntwe… but we soldier on! It’s a moving train & it is unstoppable… if not us, then who?"

Cornet's inspiration behind the nature resort

In an exclusive interview with Briefly News, Cornet Mamabolo spoke about his love for the village people and the children being one of his inspirations to open the resort.

"I was inspired by the space, the love for my village & kids. The space is more of a heritage space, but more than anything, it was to give kids in my village an amazing space to create beautiful memories. "This for me also is to say, we shouldn’t look too far for beauty, for opportunities, because they are right before us, in the very dusty villages. The inspiration is just endless, I could make references to a million things."

Cornet then also spoke about his passion for the things he does, which fuels him to succeed.

"It’s the love I have for everything I do, I do things I love & things I see I can give life to. I enjoy serving to build, and naturally, I am able to balance in most spaces. Following my heart always helps me find balance."
