TV Show Host Judge Joe Brown was born in Washington, DC, United States on July 5, 1947. He's 76 years old today.

He is most known for his work in the Criminal Court of Shelby County, Tennessee, and as the star of the television court show Judge Joe Show. While serving as the judge in the final appeal of convicted Civil Rights Leader Martin Luther King, Jr. killer Criminal James Earl Ray, he rose to national prominence.
