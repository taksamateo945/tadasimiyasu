Jenny McCarthy Gets Foot Tattoo During Super Bowl Blackout

Jenny McCarthy made the most of the Super Bowl blackout Sunday, Feb. 3. Rather than waiting around and watching the commentary for the 34-minute game delay, McCarthy, 40, went out and got some new ink!

“Got a new tattoo during half time blackout,” she tweeted with a photo of the new flower tattoo on the top of her foot. “So excited to be wearing heels on The Today Show this morning. Ouch.” She also admitted, “Hurts a bit today.”

The Jenny McCarthy Show star showed off the ink job while visiting Kathie Lee Gifford and Hoda Kotb on Monday to promote her new late night talk show.
