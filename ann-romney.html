Ann Romney Biography

Her parents, Edward Roderick Davies and Lois (Pottinger) Davies, raised her. She was raised alongside her two brothers. Her father was an entrepreneur and the town’s mayor.

She attended Kingswood School, an all-girls private school. Ann attended Brigham Young University following her high school graduation in 1967. During the second semester of her freshman year, she attended the University of Grenoble in France.
