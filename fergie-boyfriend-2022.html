Is The Singer/Rapper Married, Dating, Or Single?

Fergie and Josh Duhamel announced their divorce after eight years of what appeared to be a perfect marriage. Meanwhile, Josh has found his new love, Audra Mari. Fans were shocked by their breakup and are now searching the internet to see if Fergie has found a new boyfriend in 2022.

Stacy Ann Ferguson (b. March 27, 1975) is an American singer, rapper, and actress. She first rose to prominence as a member of the hip hop group the Black Eyed Peas. Her debut solo album, The Dutchess, was a commercial success, with three number one singles on the Billboard Hot 100: London Bridge, Glamorous, and Big Girls Don't Cry. She does not consider herself a rapper, despite vocally incorporating rapping into some of her performances.

Fergie was a member of the children's television show Kids Incorporated as well as the girl group Wild Orchid. She continued to work with the Black Eyed Peas, releasing two more albums with them, The E.N.D. and The Beginning. Moreover, she has also appeared in a number of films, including Poseidon, Grindhouse, and Nine. In May 2010, she released her first fragrance, Outspoken, through Avon, and she has since released four more fragrances. Likewise, Fergie Footwear, her footwear line, debuted in 2009.

People are interested in Fergie for reasons other than her music. Celebrity dating rumors are common, and her fans are curious about her relationship status.
